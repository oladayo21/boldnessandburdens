How to Prepare for BBC 2026

Dear Beloved,

As Boldness and Burdens Conference (BBC) approaches, we encourage you to begin preparing intentionally — both spiritually and practically — for the meeting.

BBC 2026, themed School of the Spirit, will be a time of prayer, worship, fellowship, teaching, and diverse supernatural encounters.

Here are a few ways to prepare for the conference:

  • Pray intentionally for the meeting
  • Come with expectation and spiritual hunger
  • Prepare your heart to receive from God and also bless others

The conference date remains July 16–19, in Berlin, Germany.

Please take note of the following important information:

Accommodation will be provided free of charge.

One meal per day will be provided during the conference. You are welcome to bring snacks, drinks, and additional food items if desired.

Branded T-shirts will be available for purchase. Pricing details will be shared closer to the conference. If you are interested in purchasing a T-shirt, kindly send your preferred size ahead of time.

Please come with your personal essentials, including:

  • Towel
  • Blanket
  • Soap / Shower Gel
  • Toothbrush & Toothpaste
  • Personal hygiene items
  • Bible, notebook and pen

Most importantly, come expectant. We believe God desires to meet with us in a special way at BBC.
