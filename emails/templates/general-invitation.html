Boldness and Burdens Conference (BBC) is an annual spiritual gathering where believers come together to worship God, pray, fellowship, and experience diverse supernatural encounters.

Each year is intentional and focused on a specific theme.

This year, our theme is School of the Spirit — centred on understanding how to effectively flow in the Spirit and grow in spiritual sensitivity and maturity.

BBC is open to anyone seeking deeper spiritual growth.

From July 16–19, 2026, we will gather in Berlin, Germany, for three nights dedicated to worship, prayer, fellowship, and Spirit-led encounters.

Attendance is free but registration is compulsory.
